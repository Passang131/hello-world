ABOUT ADMISSOION

For Engineering Programes

Class XII pass with pass students with a minimum of 50% each in Physics, Chemistry, Math, English and pass in Dzongkha. A CTC test will be administered. Ability Ratings: Math (5), Physics (5), Chemistry (4), English (3) and one other subject (1)

For Architectect Programes

Class XII pass with pass students with a minimum of 50% each in Physics, Chemistry, Math, English and pass in Dzongkha. An aptitude test will be administered. Students will have to achieve a minimum of 50% in the test to be eligible for the programme. Ability Ratings: Math (5), Physics (5), Chemistry (2), English (3) and Aptitude Test
